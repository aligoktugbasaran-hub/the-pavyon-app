# Privacy Policy for The Pavyon

**Last updated: March 05, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Camera Access
Our application requires access to your device's **Camera**. This permission is used exclusively for:
* Enabling video calls between users in private rooms.
* Allowing users to take profile pictures.

We **do not** record, store, or share your camera stream on our servers without your explicit actions within the app.

### Microphone Access
Our application requires access to your **Microphone** to enable audio during video calls.

## 2. Personal Data
The Pavyon is designed for anonymous social interaction. We only collect:
* Nickname (chosen by you).
* Profile pictures (uploaded by you).
* Basic device information for push notifications.
